Deliver items and earn better with Avantaland

Follow these steps to successfully deliver items using the Avantaland app:

Sign up and set up your Pal profile

Download the Avantaland app, sign up, and complete your profile with necessary details.

Receive delivery requests

Get notified of new delivery requests in your area and accept the ones that fit your schedule.

Pick up the item

Follow the provided instructions to pick up the item from the sender's location.

Confirm pickup

Use the app to confirm that you have picked up the item and are on your way to the destination.

Deliver the item

Follow the navigation and delivery instructions to deliver the item to the recipient.

Complete the delivery

Mark the delivery as complete in the app and receive your payment for the service.

By following these steps, you can ensure a smooth and efficient delivery process with Avantaland.
