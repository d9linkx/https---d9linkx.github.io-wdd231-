Business Spotlights

Greenfields Bakery

After opening their doors in 2015, Greenfields Bakery struggled to stand out in a saturated market. By partnering with the Chamber, they gained access to exclusive local events and marketing support and a 30% increase per year in foot traffic, and their custom cakes became the talk of the town.

Harper's Artisan Goods

Harper's Artisan Goods, a handcrafted home decor brand, joined the Aerodrome in 2018 to tap into the local community. With our mentorship and networking opportunities, Harper’s established strong relationships with fellow businesses and became a leader in the local craft scene, increasing sales significantly.

TechWave Solutions

TechWave Solutions, an IT consulting firm, wanted to expand beyond their small client base when they joined the Chamber in 2019. Thanks to the Chamber’s guidance, TechWave expanded their team, opened a second office, and saw a 45% increase in annual revenue. TechWave Soltions is now a recognized name in the region.
