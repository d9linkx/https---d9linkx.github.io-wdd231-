Site name: Avantaland

Avantaland Peer-to-Peer Logistics is designed to connect senders, delivery Pals, and receivers. This name emphasizes the collaborative nature of the service.

Optional domain availability: avantalandp2p.com

Site purpose

The purpose of this website is to facilitate a seamless logistics experience by allowing individuals to pick up and deliver items during their daily routes. This platform serves as a hub for everyone.

For Senders

Senders can easily request deliveries, track their items, and find cost-effective solutions.

For Pals (Delivery Persons)

Pals can browse delivery requests that fit their routes, ensuring they earn rewards while helping others.

For Receivers

Receivers can receive items quickly and affordably while tracking their deliveries in real-time.

Wireframe

Mobile View

A simple vertical layout with a hamburger menu for navigation, showcasing senders' requests, Pals' deliveries, and receivers' tracking.

Desktop View

A grid layout featuring sections for senders, Pals, and receivers, with a fixed navigation bar at the top and a footer with quick links.

CSS plan

The styling will ensure that the platform is visually appealing and adheres to the selected color schema. A responsive design will be implemented to accommodate various devices.
